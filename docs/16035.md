## 钢笔工具简介

钢笔工具，通常我们在 photoshop 中看到，它帮助我们创建一个路径和选择字符。钢笔工具也是 after effects 中一个很棒的表现工具；使用钢笔工具，我们可以轻松地绘制形状，并创建不同的遮罩；遮罩用于在后期效果中合并多层。在钢笔工具的帮助下，我们可以绘制形状和创建动画的路径；我们现在可以用钢笔工具制作动画、文本动画和形状动画，让我们看看钢笔工具帮助下的一些动画。

**第二步:**我们可以通过改变开始和结束的位置，用关键帧动画来动画化物体；这是任何人都能做到的基本正常程序。

![normal keyframe](../Images/e5633245c5ef64b683895135dfce70b1.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-224949" src="../Images/e5633245c5ef64b683895135dfce70b1.png" alt="normal keyframe" width="1319" height="742" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/normal-keyframe.jpg 1319w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/normal-keyframe-300x169.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/normal-keyframe-768x432.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/normal-keyframe-1024x576.jpg 1024w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/normal-keyframe-1280x720.jpg 1280w" sizes="(max-width: 1319px) 100vw, 1319px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/normal-keyframe.jpg"/></noscript>

但是，如果你需要做一些不同的或有创意的动画，只需通过钢笔工具。

第三步:选择我们要为动画绘制路径的形状层。

![select shape layer](../Images/78d7acb91ad6b1fef415678205e80947.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-224951" src="../Images/78d7acb91ad6b1fef415678205e80947.png" alt="select shape layer" width="1319" height="662" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-shape-layer.jpg 1319w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-shape-layer-300x151.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-shape-layer-768x385.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-shape-layer-1024x514.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1319px) 100vw, 1319px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-shape-layer.jpg"/></noscript>

**第四步:**选择钢笔工具绘制形状。

![use pen tool](../Images/4f6da8ea6dadb6e0322c5d905ad32fbe.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-224955" src="../Images/4f6da8ea6dadb6e0322c5d905ad32fbe.png" alt="use pen tool" width="1319" height="662" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/use-pen-tool.jpg 1319w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/use-pen-tool-300x151.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/use-pen-tool-768x385.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/use-pen-tool-1024x514.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1319px) 100vw, 1319px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/use-pen-tool.jpg"/></noscript>

**第五步:**现在根据我们的动画要求绘制一个形状，我们已经导入了一个球对象，所以我绘制了一个上下的形状，因为我觉得球需要从上往下下落并一步一步地跳跃，所以我从顶部的第一个点开始，绘制了一个上下的形状，并在球需要停止的角落处结束。

![animate with pen tool](../Images/9a22dd5a7854de2702057a79e54cfee0.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-224957" src="../Images/9a22dd5a7854de2702057a79e54cfee0.png" alt="animate with pen tool" width="1319" height="662" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/animate-with-pen-tool.jpg 1319w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/animate-with-pen-tool-300x151.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/animate-with-pen-tool-768x385.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/animate-with-pen-tool-1024x514.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1319px) 100vw, 1319px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/animate-with-pen-tool.jpg"/></noscript>

这里你可以看到形状是怎样的；在这个形状中，球将要运动，你可以找到球运动出屏幕的第一个点。

**第六步:**对于每一个物体，都会有一个中心点；在这个过程中，我们需要将中心点改为底部，因为我们有一个对象球；如果球在接触地面时在中间有一个中心点，它的中心点将在看起来不专业的地方遇到地面，所以我改变了中心点。如果我们有任何其他对象，我们可以根据要求改变中心点。

![change center point](../Images/b4f00e886e7e57d255c6a4552ad4ed39.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-224968" src="../Images/b4f00e886e7e57d255c6a4552ad4ed39.png" alt="change center point" width="1319" height="742" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/change-center-point.jpg 1319w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/change-center-point-300x169.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/change-center-point-768x432.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/change-center-point-1024x576.jpg 1024w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/change-center-point-1280x720.jpg 1280w" sizes="(max-width: 1319px) 100vw, 1319px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/change-center-point.jpg"/></noscript>

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-224975" src="../Images/957825b80aa82ce972ff5bdcaaba720b.png" alt="copy the path" width="1319" height="742" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/copy-the-path.jpg 1319w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/copy-the-path-300x169.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/copy-the-path-768x432.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/copy-the-path-1024x576.jpg 1024w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/copy-the-path-1280x720.jpg 1280w" sizes="(max-width: 1319px) 100vw, 1319px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/copy-the-path.jpg"/></noscript>

![select position (pen tool in after effects)](../Images/a7ec473b3ff59a8c5a66c7c56b63ecbd.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-224991" src="../Images/a7ec473b3ff59a8c5a66c7c56b63ecbd.png" alt="select position (pen tool in after effects)" width="1319" height="742" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-position-pen-tool-in-after-effects.jpg 1319w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-position-pen-tool-in-after-effects-300x169.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-position-pen-tool-in-after-effects-768x432.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-position-pen-tool-in-after-effects-1024x576.jpg 1024w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-position-pen-tool-in-after-effects-1280x720.jpg 1280w" sizes="(max-width: 1319px) 100vw, 1319px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/select-position-pen-tool-in-after-effects.jpg"/></noscript>

现在球是隐藏的，因为在将路径复制到位置应用到球时，它在该路径中移动。

### 结论

在这个过程中，我们使用钢笔工具制作一个球的动画；我们绘制了一个形状，创建了一个路径，并复制了形状层的路径，并粘贴在对象的位置，使对象跟随形状的位置。通过使用这个作为样本，我们创建了许多其他的动画。同样，我们可以使用钢笔工具来制作任何对象的动画。
