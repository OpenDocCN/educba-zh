## 什么是 AutoCAD？

AutoCAD 是一种绘图软件，建筑师、设计工程师可以在它的帮助下以 2D 3D 格式绘制设计。这是第一个帮助我们以数字格式绘制设计的软件。在此之前，人们习惯于在不同大小的图纸上画画。他们使用 A1、A2、A3 和 A4 大小的纸张来绘制对象。我们主要关注的是如何在本演示中在 Auto CAD 中插入图像。在本主题中，我们将学习在 AutoCAD 中插入图像。

### 如何在 AutoCAD 中插入图像？

在 Auto CAD 中插入图像的步骤。

#### 流程#1

**第 1 步–**点击窗口上作为快捷方式提供的 AutoCAD 图标，打开 AutoCAD 软件。AutoCAD 软件将出现在您的屏幕上，如下所示。

![Insert image in AutoCAD 1](../Images/764a135706ebc6cc636afe8b36d5c8dc.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-214780" src="../Images/764a135706ebc6cc636afe8b36d5c8dc.png" alt="Insert image in AutoCAD 1" width="979" height="584" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-1.jpg 979w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-1-300x179.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-1-768x458.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 979px) 100vw, 979px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-1.jpg"/></noscript>

**步骤 2-**在 Auto CAD 命令框中输入命令图像，然后按回车键。屏幕上将出现一个对话框，如图所示。

![Insert image in AutoCAD 2](../Images/d78f96a1917bf8143fe753d458299041.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-214781" src="../Images/d78f96a1917bf8143fe753d458299041.png" alt="Insert image in AutoCAD 2" width="311" height="629" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-2.jpg 311w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-2-148x300.jpg 148w" sizes="(max-width: 311px) 100vw, 311px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-2.jpg"/></noscript>

**第 3 步–**然后点击出现在对话框最左上角的 DWG 文件夹。会出现一个小对话框，上面有许多选项，如附加 DWG、附加图像、附加 DWF 等。单击对话框上的附加图像选项。

![Insert image in AutoCAD 3](../Images/761cb7d87187c4fcbe85559156081842.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-214783" src="../Images/761cb7d87187c4fcbe85559156081842.png" alt="Insert image in AutoCAD 3" width="1676" height="1008" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-3.jpg 1676w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-3-300x180.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-3-768x462.jpg 768w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-3-1024x616.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 1676px) 100vw, 1676px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-3.jpg"/></noscript>

**步骤 4—**屏幕上会出现一个弹出对话框。在 Auto CAD 软件上浏览你需要的图像。

![Insert image in AutoCAD 4](../Images/1374ad12904bdfc4c84e851160f51795.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-214785" src="../Images/1374ad12904bdfc4c84e851160f51795.png" alt="Insert image in AutoCAD 4" width="661" height="518" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-4.jpg 661w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-4-300x235.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 661px) 100vw, 661px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-4.jpg"/></noscript>

**第五步—**你浏览完之后会弹出一个对话框，会询问你需要的图片的路径类型，插入点，缩放，旋转。将路径类型指定为“完整”,并按“确定”,将所有其他选项指定为“在屏幕上指定”。这样，您可以在 Auto CAD 窗口的任何位置插入图像。

![Insert image in AutoCAD 5](../Images/339b3c243278084269a8319c3d35ad4d.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-214786" src="../Images/339b3c243278084269a8319c3d35ad4d.png" alt="Insert image in AutoCAD 5" width="628" height="370" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-5.jpg 628w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-5-300x177.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 628px) 100vw, 628px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-5.jpg"/></noscript>

**步骤 6-**如果您知道图像的确切位置和比例因子，那么您可以按如下所示输入位置和比例因子。

![Insert image in AutoCAD 6](../Images/c7aa406c24c0bdcca00ef4bd9e19203f.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-214788" src="../Images/c7aa406c24c0bdcca00ef4bd9e19203f.png" alt="Insert image in AutoCAD 6" width="635" height="372" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-6.jpg 635w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-6-300x176.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 635px) 100vw, 635px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/09/Insert-image-in-AutoCAD-6.jpg"/></noscript>

**第 7 步-**指定插入位置和旋转后，图像将出现在屏幕上，如图所示。

![Step 7](../Images/f3183a376fc7f8c338495f36b1e7cd1d.png)

**第 8 步–**您也可以按照上述流程在同一窗口输入多幅图像。

#### 流程#2

您可以通过单击“插入”任务栏上的“附加”图标直接插入图像，并遵循步骤 5 到步骤 8 的相同过程。

![Process 2](../Images/295aadbea7d1c68c8e69476c5d89a83c.png)
