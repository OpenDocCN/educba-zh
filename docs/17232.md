## Photoshop 中的雪花效果介绍

Photoshop 是广泛使用的工具，用于在照片中添加多种效果和属性。它首先被选为图形编辑、图像效果添加、杂志制作、新闻页面内的文章框架等项目的项目管道。我们可以给图像添加不真实的外观和场景，比如降雪、降雨、阳光调整、锐度、模糊、颜色调整、气流等等。今天我们将看到如何给照片添加雪的效果。照片的选择也起着关键的作用，所以选择最适合雪效果的照片，并按照下面的步骤使用 photoshop 图层将雪效果添加到照片上。这些类似的技巧可以应用到其他合适的照片上。

### 如何在 Photoshop 中创建雪花效果？

**步骤 1:** 添加一个新的黑色“雪”层被添加。

![Snow Effect in Photoshop step 1](../Images/63703eeb0341492d8d486fcb090109cf.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189660" src="../Images/63703eeb0341492d8d486fcb090109cf.png" alt="Snow Effect in Photoshop step 1" width="619" height="555" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-1.jpg 619w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-1-300x269.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 619px) 100vw, 619px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-1.jpg"/></noscript>

在 Photoshop 应用程序中打开必须添加雪效果的照片。从图层中，选择一个新图层图标创建一个新的空白图层到图层面板，命名为可记忆。在 windows 中按住 **Alt** 键，在 Mac OS 中按住 **Option** 键。

**步骤 2:** 点击编辑按钮，从菜单栏中选择填充选项。

![Snow Effect in Photoshop step 2](../Images/793fcc931dee50a1daafca0d6a051866.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189662" src="../Images/793fcc931dee50a1daafca0d6a051866.png" alt="Snow Effect in Photoshop step 2" width="668" height="647" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-Effect-in-Photoshop-step-2.jpg 668w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-Effect-in-Photoshop-step-2-300x291.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 668px) 100vw, 668px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-Effect-in-Photoshop-step-2.jpg"/></noscript>

选择新创建的层，并用黑色填充。进入编辑菜单**。**

**第三步:**填充选项。

![Snow Effect in Photoshop step 3](../Images/b3e796ee8addd89c12a6de9f7dd8b1f2.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189664" src="../Images/b3e796ee8addd89c12a6de9f7dd8b1f2.png" alt="Snow Effect in Photoshop step 3" width="672" height="599" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-Effect-in-Photoshop-step-3.jpg 672w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-Effect-in-Photoshop-step-3-300x267.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 672px) 100vw, 672px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Snow-Effect-in-Photoshop-step-3.jpg"/></noscript>

将打开一个对话框，详细说明从下拉列表中选择黑色并点击确定。新创建的层将用黑色填充。

**第四步:**进入滤镜，从菜单栏选择噪波和新增噪波选项。

![Noise option Step - 4](../Images/aa0a55c2954338bb7dd5cd6d2fb95a01.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189666" src="../Images/aa0a55c2954338bb7dd5cd6d2fb95a01.png" alt="Noise option Step - 4" width="828" height="615" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Noise-option.jpg 828w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Noise-option-300x223.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Noise-option-768x570.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 828px) 100vw, 828px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Noise-option.jpg"/></noscript>

选择一个黑色层，从菜单栏中选择过滤器，悬停到噪声，并选择添加噪声选项。新的窗口将被打开与添加噪声选项，和一些配置一样，数额必须设置为 25。从分布中，启用高斯以启用单色，然后单击确定。我们可以看到一些白点添加到层。填充噪点后根据需要调整亮度。

![BLack card](../Images/b7b0a5ca1b3d09e9e9d468eae28086cb.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189667" src="../Images/b7b0a5ca1b3d09e9e9d468eae28086cb.png" alt="BLack card" width="823" height="631" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/BLack-card.jpg 823w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/BLack-card-300x230.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/BLack-card-768x589.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 823px) 100vw, 823px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/BLack-card.jpg"/></noscript>

**第五步:**进入编辑，点击变换按钮，选择缩放选项。

**![tranform Scale Step 5](../Images/b610264cde95863c010ebad4bc863337.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189669" src="../Images/b610264cde95863c010ebad4bc863337.png" alt="tranform Scale Step 5" width="832" height="722" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/tranform-Scale-Step-5.jpg 832w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/tranform-Scale-Step-5-300x260.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/tranform-Scale-Step-5-768x666.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 832px) 100vw, 832px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/tranform-Scale-Step-5.jpg"/></noscript>** 

我们需要使白色尺寸更大。为此，从菜单栏转到编辑，选择变换并选择缩放，单击链接(-)选项，根据需要设置宽度和高度值。当发生变化时，此链接会自动调整宽度和高度。全球建议的宽度和高度为 400%。

单击选项栏中的复选标记接受更改。通过这样做，白点增加更大，看起来像雪。这一步必须做，只有当它是必要的图像建议大图片，而不是较小的，接近或肖像图片。

第六步:图层混合模式>屏幕

![Normal Scale step 6](../Images/9b5131c464d082c082803df387277684.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189675" src="../Images/9b5131c464d082c082803df387277684.png" alt="Normal Scale step 6" width="242" height="206" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Normal-Scale-step-6.jpg"/></noscript>

假设我们识别出噪声中的任何问题，这些问题将被完全去除，这将使我们的图像获得更持久的所需视图。选择层，改变混合模式为屏幕从默认到屏幕。

![Screen Step 6.1](../Images/0d8048e59648bf8fe1b0efa35963e2bf.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189680" src="../Images/0d8048e59648bf8fe1b0efa35963e2bf.png" alt="Screen Step 6.1" width="831" height="644" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Screen-Step-6.1.jpg 831w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Screen-Step-6.1-300x232.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Screen-Step-6.1-768x595.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 831px) 100vw, 831px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Screen-Step-6.1.jpg"/></noscript>

通过这样做，屏幕混合模式隐藏了图层的黑色和图像上可见的白噪音。我们的背景原始出现了雪层的白噪音。

**步骤 7:** 进入滤镜，点击模糊选项，选择运动模糊

![Motion Blur Step 7](../Images/1c90f5d632b80e0992cdbc4558d79b87.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189681" src="../Images/1c90f5d632b80e0992cdbc4558d79b87.png" alt="Motion Blur Step 7" width="833" height="664" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Motion-Blur-Step-7.jpg 833w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Motion-Blur-Step-7-300x239.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Motion-Blur-Step-7-768x612.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 833px) 100vw, 833px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Motion-Blur-Step-7.jpg"/></noscript>

从菜单栏转到滤镜并选择模糊，然后选择运动模糊**。**这有助于将运动模糊滤镜应用于图层，以获得降雪效果，为雪添加自然的外观和感觉。

步骤 8: 进入滤镜，点击运动图层，调整角度

![(How to create Snow Effect in Photoshop) Step 8](../Images/5cc6c4e4b9f0f871f2645aff51bdaf92.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone wp-image-189682 size-full" src="../Images/5cc6c4e4b9f0f871f2645aff51bdaf92.png" alt="(How to create Snow Effect in Photoshop) Step 8" width="825" height="695" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Angle-Step-8.jpg 825w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Angle-Step-8-300x253.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Angle-Step-8-768x647.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 825px) 100vw, 825px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Angle-Step-8.jpg"/></noscript>

运动模糊窗口，角度-65，像素距离为 16 到 20，点击确定。这将为图层中的白色像素添加降雪角度和运动。

**第九步:**添加可调图层。

![Adjusting layer Step 9](../Images/3fe6ed6231d4f3227b69a650a0104c51.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189683" src="../Images/3fe6ed6231d4f3227b69a650a0104c51.png" alt="Adjusting layer Step 9" width="816" height="634" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Adjusting-layer-Step-9.jpg 816w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Adjusting-layer-Step-9-300x233.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Adjusting-layer-Step-9-768x597.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 816px) 100vw, 816px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Adjusting-layer-Step-9.jpg"/></noscript>

如果需要，我们可以使用调整层来降低雪的效果或亮度。单击“图层”面板按钮上的“新建填充或调整图层”图标。管理 RGB 值和其他属性也取决于我们作为背景的图像颜色。

**第十步:**转到图层，点击可调图层，选择选项级别。

![Step 10 - layer Level](../Images/11eb4fd2a92bd17cf2d4c9e48eca34a3.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189684" src="../Images/11eb4fd2a92bd17cf2d4c9e48eca34a3.png" alt="Step 10 - layer Level" width="827" height="699" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Step-10-layer-Level.jpg 827w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Step-10-layer-Level-300x254.jpg 300w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Step-10-layer-Level-768x649.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 827px) 100vw, 827px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Step-10-layer-Level.jpg"/></noscript>

从菜单栏中选择图层转到图层，然后转到图层选择新的可调整图层，然后选择级别并将输入级别调整为 162 1.00 204 或 41 1.00 119，这是常用的级别。级别的值还会根据拍摄的图像延迟，并按所需的速率调整降雪量。然后点击 ok 来应用雪的效果。

![Step 10.1- Custom](../Images/72ed18159e71e107ac5f3c13530c8b7b.png)

<noscript><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-189687" src="../Images/72ed18159e71e107ac5f3c13530c8b7b.png" alt="Step 10.1- Custom" width="683" height="536" srcset="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Step-10.1-Custom.jpg 683w, https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Step-10.1-Custom-300x235.jpg 300w" sizes="(max-width: 683px) 100vw, 683px" data-original-src="https://cdn.educba.com/academy/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/Step-10.1-Custom.jpg"/></noscript>

### 结论

photoshop 中的 Snow effect 有助于将雪的观感添加到所需的图像中；这种方法有助于以同样的方式和程序为照片添加非季节性效果。总是很难在照片上产生这样的效果，并通过轻微的风景调整来使雪的效果与拍摄的照片相匹配。如果我们按照上面的步骤，很容易在一天结束时给图像添加雪的效果。我们学习了如何根据图像的要求制作雪花，改变雪花的角度来获得雪花落下的真实感。完整的课程详细介绍了使用不同的视图和属性过程来个性化图片，以及使用专业技巧为选定的图像添加雪花效果。尝试这些步骤，让你的形象变得清晰。
